Academics

We provide credit for prior learning, professional and secondary experience, and more with our flexible transfer credit choices across our more than 20 undergraduate programs. Additionally, we provide more than 41 graduate programs, including online doctoral programs, certificates, master's degrees, and specializations. Lumos University has been named the best Magic Learning Schools in America for 2022. Numerous of our graduate programs, including those in Herbology, Defence Against the Dark Arts, Wand Crafting, Alchemy, and other fields, have won honors and praise for their innovation, and accessibility. Additionally, we provide lifetime career services and resources for all students and graduates, including feedback on resume-building, job and internship postings, mentorship opportunities, webinars, and more.

Majors

Getting a bachelor's degree is a significant step in your educational, professional, and personal development. The goal of Lumos University is to make it as simple as possible for you to obtain your degree.

Electives

Electives can help you stand out from the competition, regardless of whether you're just starting your career or want to improve your leadership and skill set in your existing position.

Continuing Education

Whether you are looking for a new job, looking to advance your career, learning a skill or exploring a new hobby, what unites us in our school of continuing education is an absolute passion for personal growth and improvement.
