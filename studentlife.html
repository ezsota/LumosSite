Student Life

One of the best parts of the Lumos University experience is living in one of the 12 student residences on campus. Each hall has its own character and traditions and often offers a community where you can connect with other students who share your major and interests. You can't spend all your time studying or in the classroom. That's why Lumos University offers so many ways to stay in shape, relax, and have a little fun, whether you're flying your broom, climbing the wall or cheering for the Lumineers.

Athletics

Lumos University is home to an active and competitive intercollegiate athletic program. With 4 athletic programs for men and women, there is something for everyone. Join a sports team or cheer for the Lumineers. Let's go Lumineers!

Clubs

If you have a special interest or just want to make friends outside of class, why not join a student organization? Lumos University has over 10 student organizations where you can meet fellow students who share your curiosity and enthusiasm it offers.

Events

From sporting events to graduations, educational conferences to dance performances and concerts, we have the facilities and staff to meet your needs. We welcome community groups, nonprofits, schools, businesses, and public agencies.
